Metamer Task

Welcome to the Metamer experiment!

In this experiment, your task will be asked to determine if two images are the same.
You will first fixate on a cross, then be shown 3 images.

The goal is to determine if the last image is the same as the first or second image. Please use the keys f, s for first and second, respectively.
Please answer quickly, and as accurately as you can.

Please press the space bar to continue.
