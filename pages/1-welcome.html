Metamer Task

Welcome to the Metamer experiment!

In this experiment, you will be asked to determine if two images are the same.
You will first fixate on a cross, then be shown 3 images in quick succession.
You have to maintain your eyes' fixation on where the cross was throughout the presentation of these 3 images. That is, try not to move your eyes throughout the presentation of the images.

The goal is to determine if the last (third) image is the same as the first or second image. Please use the keys f, s for first and second, respectively.
Please answer quickly, and as accurately as you can.

Please press the space bar to continue.
